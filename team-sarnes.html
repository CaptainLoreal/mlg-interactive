Kristof
Sarnes

Munich Leadership Group

Kristof holds a Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering from Ostfalia and a Master's in Industrial Engineering from TU Braunschweig, complemented by additional training in process design, facilitation, and agile development. He has worked across automotive, chemical, semiconductor, and public sector industries, and brings rare entrepreneurial experience from a moonshot robotics start-up and building an intrapreneurial workshop and consulting unit from scratch.

Described as an authentic motivator and innovator, Kristof's distinctive contribution to MLG is his ability to make transformation genuinely exciting. He believes that real change happens when people feel trusted to take ownership — and that authenticity, not performance, is the foundation of lasting team culture.

His work draws on the Extreme Ownership leadership model, agile methods, and a deep belief in trust as the central driver of team performance. He designs and facilitates learning journeys that challenge leaders to think and act differently — not just in workshops, but back in their day-to-day roles.

Kristof is also an expert in holistic innovation management: from MVP development and prototyping to social media strategy and digital transformation. He brings an engineer's precision and an entrepreneur's energy to every engagement.
