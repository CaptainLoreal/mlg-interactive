What we offer

Coaching & Sparring formats

Performance Coaching

You might have the feeling that things are getting overwhelming. Maybe your energy fades. Maybe you are struggling with your team too often, and your environment might demand too much from you.

Or you are curious and you feel there are some hidden potentials in you that you want to unleash.

Our Performance Coaching will help you manage your energy, gain and implement new ideas and demonstrate new behaviors.

Development Coaching

When you step up the career ladder, or your area of responsibility gets broader, you will be confronted with new challenges: value-based conflicts, power play, delegation, dealing with extended responsibility, networking. What about reflecting these development challenges with an expert?

Our Development Coaching will support you to take the next step in your personal growth.

Transition Coaching

You are a high performer – that is why you have been assigned a new role in a new environment. What are the most important actions now? How do you deal with uncertainty around you? And what are the chances during the first 100 days you should not miss?

Our Transition Coaching makes you ready to perform best in your new role from day one.

Executive Sparring

As a top executive, you have mastered so many critical situations. You have developed into a most successful personality. But still there are sensitive topics in your life that you would like to reflect with an expert on eye-level. Somebody who understands your business challenges as well as your "human system".

Our Executive Sparring reflects your challenges with you – on top level, flexible, 24/7.
