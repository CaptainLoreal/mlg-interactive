Management Team

Martina
Bohnenstiel

Munich Leadership Group

Martina holds a Diploma in Business Administration from the European Business School (ebs) in Oestrich-Winkel, with study periods in London and Paris. She is a certified Systemic Coach (DBVC) and has built a diverse career spanning new media, consulting, telecommunications, and distance selling.

As a responsible line manager and team leader, she brings real-world credibility to her work in leadership facilitation and coaching. She has designed and delivered HR and organisational development programmes for major organisations, and has a particular talent for facilitation: bringing groups to clarity, alignment, and action in strategy and target agreement workshops.

Martina's work covers the full spectrum of people development — from HR strategy and talent management through to personal branding and team realignment. She is especially effective in change processes where the human dimension is most critical: where culture must shift, performance must improve, and people must genuinely buy in rather than simply comply.

Her perspective on change is rooted in a powerful insight: that our perception is limited by our prior experience. Genuine transformation requires openness to perspectives we haven't considered yet — and the courage to question assumptions that once served us well.

Core insight
