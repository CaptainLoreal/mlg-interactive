Team / Klaus Dürrbeck

Klaus brings an uncommon background to leadership development: a 12-year career as an officer in the German Armed Forces, including deployments to Spain, Italy, Denmark, Norway, Afghanistan, Lithuania, and the Netherlands. As a Public Affairs Officer and leadership trainer, he developed first-hand expertise in high-stakes decision-making, team cohesion under pressure, and the development of future leaders.

He holds a double degree in International Business Administration from Hochschule München, Tampere University of Applied Sciences (Finland), and St. Petersburg State Polytechnic University (Russia), with majors in Change Management and Entrepreneurship. He completed his coaching training at the renowned Coaches Training Institute (CTI).

Klaus specialises in developing the next generation of leaders — Generations Y and Z — and is a sought-after partner for organisations navigating digital transformation, neuroleadership, and innovation. His military background lends him a unique ability to engage leaders who need to perform under real pressure, while his entrepreneurial training keeps his approach forward-looking and practical.
