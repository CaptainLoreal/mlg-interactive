Christine
Westermayr

Munich Leadership Group

Christine holds a Master's degree in Business Administration and Economics from the University of Augsburg (cum Laude) and a Wealth Management Diploma from UBS AG in Zürich. She is a NeuroColor Certified Lead Facilitator for Europe and an Erickson Certified Professional Coach in Neuro Linguistic Programming.

Her career spans an impressive range of contexts: from UBS Wealth Management managing an asset portfolio exceeding one billion euros, to Spudy Family Office, retail, and the sports and wellness industries. She led the successful European market entry of Lululemon, the Canadian sportswear brand, and has deep entrepreneurial experience in building new markets from the ground up.

Christine is a specialist in curiosity-based transformation — helping organisations and leaders navigate change not through fear or compliance, but through genuine engagement with what's possible. Her psychometric expertise in NeuroColor gives her a sophisticated lens on neurodiversity and how different thinking styles can be leveraged as a performance advantage.

She excels in high performance coaching with a particular focus on emotional regulation: helping leaders sustain peak performance through the inevitable pressures, setbacks, and uncertainties of demanding roles. Her approach is brain-based, evidence-driven, and deeply practical.
