Managing Partner

Michael
Knieling

Munich Leadership Group

Michael holds an MBA from the University of Mannheim (1991) and brings over 25 years of experience across automotive, pharmaceutical, mechanical engineering, banking, and insurance sectors. His career began at Mercedes-Benz AG in logistics and transport, followed by strategy consulting at Ernst & Young, where he focused on Business Process Reengineering and Performance Improvement.

From 1995 to 2007 he worked as a Senior Consultant and Facilitator at SHS Business Consultants, building deep expertise in transformation design and executive facilitation. He joined Munich Leadership Group in 2008 and was appointed Managing Partner in 2020.

Michael's strength lies in his ability to initiate and guide large-scale transformation — from cultural change to leadership programme design and business process optimisation. He combines strategic thinking with hands-on coaching, and is equally at home running a board-level strategy session or coaching an individual executive through a critical inflection point.

Certified in NLP, Transactional Analysis, MBTI, StrengthsFinder, and DISC, he brings a rich psychometric toolkit to every engagement. Beyond work, he is a passionate tennis player and father of twins.
